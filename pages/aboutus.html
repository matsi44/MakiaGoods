About Us

Makia Goods is a ready-to-wear stylish accessories founded in 2022 in a small dorm room by three friends eager to create something different. Franco, Gian and Lucas were raised in completely different environments. The three of us from Patagonia, but growing up in unique circumstances that lead into different perspectives creating an instant connection that led them to create a joint movement that would change their lives forever. "For us, it was all about the adrenaline, being honest and grateful with the opportunity. The artist’s creativity which motivated us to feel and share u a way of living.”

We strive for designs that embrace a modern attitude for a creative life, seamlessly blending comfort, minimalism and elegance in every accessory.

Makia Goods is more than just accessories. It is a creative platform for the misfits, the outcasts and the wild to express themselves. Our garments aim to inspire the new generation to pursue their passion, orthodoxly or not, by empowering them to create without fear of judgement. Go on and live your life as you would like.

One of our goals is to change the perceived idea of success. We realized that focusing on money or fame just to seek acceptance, won't ever be fulfilling.

Go on and seek your passion. Some people will call it luck, but if there is something we are certain about is that the harder we work, the more luck we have.

Early on we realized the traditional path did not fit us. Following our principles we itched to start working. Because you are the only person stopping yourself from doing what you love. We want to fulfill those who want to create a change, and work hard for it.
